About Me

Hello and welcome to my page!

My name is Tony Bowman, and I am currently a second-year student at Illinois State University. I am majoring in creative technologies, heading down the audio and music production path. I love to make music, mostly vocal melodies and harmonies, but I also make some fully electronic music on the side. I am a singer in Illinois State University's premier all-bass a cappella group, in which I sing baritone two and am the current vice president. Outside of school, I love to spend time with friends and family, play guitar, and go on long walks. I hope you enjoy the rest of my page! Thank you for visiting!
